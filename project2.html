Description

As part of a networking lab, I built a Cat 6 Ethernet patch cable from scratch. I followed the T568B wiring standard, prepared the twisted pairs, crimped the connectors, and tested the cable to verify successful connectivity. This project reinforced my understanding of the physical layer and the importance of proper cabling in IT infrastructure.

Skills Learned

  • Networking fundamentals
  • Hands-on hardware assembly
  • T568B wiring standards
  • Testing and troubleshooting connectivity
  • Attention to detail in physical IT work
